Things you can do now to save the planet

  • Change yor lamps for flourescents ones. They save energy and durate 8x more!
  • Turn off electric equipments when they're not in use. Just like obvious, but we ever forget this!
  • Make trips on bus, metro, boat, bike or by feet. You save money, calories and carbonic gas!
  • Buy notes and books made in nature-friendly paper. They cost less for you and for the Earth!
  • Tell your friends about theese tips. The more people doing this, the better life will be!
